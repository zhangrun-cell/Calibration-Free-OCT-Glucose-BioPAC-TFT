# Data Availability

The clinical OCT-glucose dataset used in the manuscript contains human
measurements collected under institutional ethical approval. The raw data are
not publicly released because of institutional ethics, participant privacy, and
clinical data-use restrictions.

This repository provides:

- the Bio-PAC preprocessing implementation,
- DEJ-anchored feature construction utilities,
- prediction aggregation and evaluation scripts,
- a small anonymized/synthetic demo CSV that follows the same data conventions.

Access to de-identified clinical data may be considered by the corresponding
author under reasonable scientific request and subject to institutional ethics
approval and data-use agreements.
